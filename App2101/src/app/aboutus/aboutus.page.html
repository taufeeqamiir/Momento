About Us

Momento Mental Health Mobile Application is a mobile application that integrates mental health associations, volunteer listeners and in-platform campaigns that drive self-development and motivation.

The aim of this application is to provide a way for people who want to seek advice anonymously or just seek motivation. Although, to promote the treatment of mental illness and provide positive mental health motivation to consumers.

This app also promotes good mental health and self-motivation. This app also aims to provide a way for people who want to contact mental health advice or are just looking for motivation.
